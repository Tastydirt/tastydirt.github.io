Who are we?
Cois farraige is a family run Bed & Breakfast located in a quiet, scenic area just 1km from the centre of Lahinch town. All bedrooms ensuite and free WiFi access throughout the house with
a beautiful view of the ocean from the dining room. Large family room availible which sleeps up to 5 persons and baby cots availible on request.


Rates
Prices are €40.00 per person sharing for July and August and €35.00 per person sharing for all other months. Children sharing have 50% discount. Single is €50.00


Local Attractions
Lahinch is a small and friendly seaside village famous for it's long sandy beach and great surfing. Other attractions include a strand promenade, aquarium and swimming pool and a variety of great restaurants and cafes. Lahinch also offers plenty of different activities. Golfers can choose between two links courses, the Atlantic coast offers great fishing for anglers and surfers should find excellent conditions in one of the many great surf spots.

All of this makes Cois Farraige is the ideal base for visiting local attractions such as:
Lahinch Glof Club: www.lahinchgolf.com
Cliffs of Moher: www.cliffsofmoher.ie
The Burren: www.theburrencentre.ie
Doolin Cave: www.doolincave.ie
Aillwee Cave: www.aillweecave.ie
and many more.
